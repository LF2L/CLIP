Climate Labs Incubation Program 2021

Program Schedule

Sprint Goal(s) Main Focus

Sprint 1

Fundamentals & Principles

3 weeks

From 09/04/21 to 29/04/21

 - Build the Climate Lab Manifesto

- Set the stage in terms of Climate Change, Social Innovation and Innovation Lab


- Teams focus on defining the CLT purpose

Sprint 2

Strategic Intent & Process of Creation

4 weeks

From 30/04/21 to 27/05/21

- Define the Climate Lab’s vision and objectives


- Design of the social innovation model

- The importance of building a collective strategic intent


- Innovation labs facilitate innovation processes: what is the social innovation process for your Climate Lab?

Sprint 3

Physical Embodiment

4 weeks

From 28/05/21 to 01/07/21

 - Translate the Climate Lab’s vision into a technological resource roadmap

- Management of technological resources


- Spaces to support social innovation

Sprint 4

Process of Use & Outcomes

6 weeks (including summer break)

From 02/07/21 to 19/08/21

 - Design the business model & local strategy of the Climate Lab - The role of innovation of innovation lab in universities and climate change

Sprint 5

Assessment & Adaptation

4 weeks

From 20/08/21 to 23/09/21

 - Assess the Climate Lab performance - Practices for a more creative, resilient and enduring operation

Sprint 6

Pilot Project

3 weeks

From 24/09/21 to 15/10/21

 - Set-up of the local Climate Lab pilot project - Moving from strategic to operational level: applying the living lab approach
